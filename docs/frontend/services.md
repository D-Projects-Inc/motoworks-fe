## Overview

A comprehensive, descriptive list of services that a MotoWorks mechanic could offer, covering motorcycles, generators, and other small engine machines (like lawnmowers, water pumps, and other gas-powered tools). I’ll organize it into categories, with a brief description for each service, so you can use it directly for your website, brochures, or internal reference.

----
----

# 1. Motorcycle Services

## A. Routine Maintanance & Servicing

- **Oil & Filter Changes** – Regular engine oil and filter replacement to ensure engine longevity.
- **Brake Inspection & Replacement** – Checking pads, discs, fluids, and ensuring responsive braking.
- **Chain & Sprocket Care** – Cleaning, lubrication, adjustment, and replacement if worn.
- **Tire Maintenance** – Pressure checks, alignment, and replacement of worn tires.
- **Battery Check & Replacement** – Ensuring reliable starts and electrical system health.
- **Fluid Top-Ups** – Coolant, brake, clutch, and fork oils.

----

## B. Repairs & Diagnostics

- **Engine Diagnostics** – Using tools or manual inspection to detect engine problems.
- **Electrical Repairs** – Fixing wiring, lights, ignition issues, and charging systems.
- **Suspension Repairs** – Fork rebuilds, shock absorber replacement, and adjustment.
- **Exhaust & Muffler Repair** – Fixing leaks, rust, and noise issues.

----

## C. Performance & Customization

- **Tuning & Carburetor Adjustment** – Optimizing engine performance for efficiency or power.
- **Upgrades** – Installing performance parts, exhausts, lights, or accessories.
- **Custom Paint & Detailing** – Personalizing bikes with paint jobs or decals.

# 2. Generator & Small Engine Services

## A. Routine Maintenance

- **Oil & Filter Changes** – Essential for reliable power generation.
- **Fuel System Check & Cleaning** – Carburetor cleaning, fuel line inspection, fuel filter replacement.
- **Battery & Starter Check** – Ensuring smooth starting and battery health.
- **Load Testing** – Verifying generator outputs correct voltage and current.

----

## B. Repairs & Diagnostics

- **Engine Repairs** – Cylinder, piston, spark plug, or valve replacements.
- **Electrical Repairs** – Fixing alternators, wiring, or control panel issues.
- **Cooling System Maintenance** – Radiator cleaning, fan repairs, or coolant replacement.

----

## C. Installation & Setup

- **Home/Business Installation** – Placing and wiring generators safely.
- **Portable Generator Setup** – Ensuring correct connections and safety measures.

----
